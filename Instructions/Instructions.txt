1) Go to https://www/youtube.com/
2) Find the homepage of a creator you want to track
3) In the description area, click on the '...more' link.
4) Scroll to the bottom of the popup and click the 'Share channel' button.
5) Click on 'Copy channel ID', this will copy the id of the channel
6) Right click on the icon in Droptop for this app, this will offer the options.
7) Mouse over the feed to change starting with Box1.
8) The top box is the title, name it whatever you want, click to open, press 'ENTER' or 'ESC' key to save info.
9) The bottom box is for the copied id, paste it into this box, click to open, press 'ENTER' or 'ESC' key to save info.
10) Repeat steps 8 and 9 for up to 5 feeds.
11) If everything is correct, when you open the app, you should see the latest feed for that creator.
